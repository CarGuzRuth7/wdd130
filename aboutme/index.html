Ruth Cardona

Pantache

Hello Everyone! I'm Ruth Cardona but my "artistic" nickname is Pantache ("ch" like children), this is a word game in Spanish that means Panda and Racoon. I am 25 years old, and I'm married. I've been a member of the church my whole life. I served a mission at Temple Square in Utah, and it was a wonderful experience.

I'm studying two careers, one in Applied Technology at BYU-I and one as a lyrical singer at the local Music Conservatory. I love to study and enjoy what I do, even though sometimes it is hard. I like to draw, sing, watch anime and movies, play games and listen to music.

What I love about BYU-Idaho is giving me opportunities to grow deeper in my faith and trust in God. Now I believe more that I can become a good programmer than before. I started to study this career because I want to find better job opportunities and grow professionally. I'm currently serving as a Seminary teacher, and I enjoy spending time with the youth.
