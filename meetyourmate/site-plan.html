Overview

Purpose

Do you know what MATE is? Most people outside of Argentina, Uruguay, or South America do not know about this traditional tea. The first impression it gives is to think you are drinking grass in a cup with a straw or that you are on the dark side of the law. Through my website, I pretend to share why "mate" is considered a great life companion and how it represents unity between family, friends, and community. A unity that invites to understand and respect each other traditions

Audience

Through this website, I expect to reach people of all ages and different cultures that never heard of or have a little knowledge of what "Mate" is and want to know more about it and its benefits and tradition. Also, those who want to understand why it is considered valuable to those who drink it. I supposed them to find the page through different devices such as mobile devices, desktops, laptops, and any other with internet access.

How to Prepare Mate

How to prepare Mate:
Through this content, I will share more about how a traditional mate has to be prepared step by step to be able to drink it. It will also explain and clarify and to make especial preparation to the mate that it’s made with gourd or wood.

The steps to prepare a mate are six:

1. Heat water between 158°F to 167°F (70°C/75°c). If it’s your first time drinking mate I recommend not to drink it too hot.
2. While the water is heating take the mate cup and fill it with ¾ parts of yerba.
3. Cover the mouth of the mate with your hand and shake it. Return the mate to its original position but leave the yerba at 45°.
4. Pour a little warm water (around 122°F) into the emptiest part of the mate.
5. Insert the straw in the same place as in the previous step to the bottom of the mate.
6. Pour the hot water from the side of the straw until the mate is filled.

It is ready to drink!

Other ways to prepare Mate:
There are more ways to drink Mate if the traditional way is not what you prefer.

Sugar: Mate has a naturally bitter taste, that is the way many people in Argentina use to put a little portion of sugar each time a mate is served or in the water recipient. It is also valid to use sweetener or honey as an alternative option.

Mate with flavor: Another alternative is to add flavor to your mates, like little pieces of lemon, orange, or grapefruit.

Tereré: Especially drink it in Paraguay, tereré is a mate but with cold water. It is the best preferred in summer when the heats arise. It can also be made with juice or different natural herbs.

Mate Cocido (Boil Mate): It is Yerba Mate in a tea bag, you can also use a strainer to filter the mate tea. The only difference is that you have to boil the water before serving.

Mate with milk: As an alternative to water, you can also serve it with milk. It also works for the boil mate.

Grapefruit: You can make a mate in a grapefruit!

FAQs

In this section I will attend to the most common questions about mates:


What is Mate? Mate is called to the infusion but also is how it’s called the cup.

Is Mate a drug? No, it is not a drug but it contains caffeine, theobromine, and theophylline that stimulate the nervous system.

Who can drink mate? Anyone! It is safe to drink and it brings many benefits to the body.

What is Yerba Mate? The plant origin from the Paraná River basin and the tributaries of the Uruguay River. It grows as a wild plant but at present, there are production plantations in the area.

How often do I have to change the Yerba? Every time it loose flavor and every time you start a new mate round (after a lot of hours or after day).

Can I drink mate with boiled water? It is recommended to use water before it boils. Boil water can “burn” the yerba and yourself, and it is not good for the stomach to consume a lot of hot drinks.

What is mate good for? The Yerba Mate is a great antioxidant agent. These antioxidants help stop cell aging. It is also a source of B1 and B2 vitamins and essential minerals, etc. It has diuretic properties too.

What did Yerba Mate choose? There are many brands and types of yerba mate and everyone varies its taste. It will depend on every person to choose which one is the prefer to keep drinking. You always can try its many variations.
