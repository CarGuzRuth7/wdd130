FAQs

If is your first time you hearing about mate, here is were we will attend the most common questions about MATE.

What is Mate?

Mate is called to the infusion but also is how it’s called the cup.

Is Mate a drug?

No, is not a drug, but it contains caffeine, theobromine, and theophylline that stimulate the nervous system.

Who can drink mate?

Anyone! It is safe to drink and brings many benefits to the body.

What is Yerba Mate?

The plant origin from the Paraná River basin and the tributaries of the Uruguay River. It grows as a wild plant but at present, there are production plantations in the area.

How often do I have to change the Yerba?

Every time it loses taste, and every time you start a new mate round (after a lot of hours or after a day).

Can I drink mate with boiled water?

It is recommended to use water before it boils. Boil water can “burn” the yerba and yourself, and it is not good for the stomach to consume a lot of hot drinks.

What is mate good for?

The Yerba Mate is a great antioxidant agent. These antioxidants help stop cell aging. It is also a source of B1 and B2 vitamins, essential minerals, etc. It has diuretic properties too.

What did Yerba Mate choose?

There are many brands and types of yerba mate, and everyone varies its taste. It will depend on each person to choose which one is the preferred to keep drinking. You can try its many variations.
